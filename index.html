🥚What is egg?🥚

  • Available to join for anyone with 25 in Teleportation and 2 Aquamarines!
  • Level 90+ item rewards from bosses and elites open to all, as is Xogrite.

🥚Egg Best Practices🥚

  • Come prepared! Have your 2x Aquamarines for teleport machine, food/snack, diamonds for rez, and inventory space.
  • Get to level 45 first aid, go buy some pineal juice (NPC in serb sells it), and gift it to the fairy in sun vale to learn Resuscitate. Yes it costs money. Yes you're saving for something. So is everyone. Do your part and get this unlocked. A single egg run will more than cover this cost.
  • I said it before, but don't AOE during egg run. Leading cause of death.
  • When you see the cocks, nuke the cocks.
  • Share the harvestables along the route - don't hog them all.
  • If you are new, let experienced people pull.
  • Dying a lot? We all have bad days. Stick to the back and save your friends diamonds.

🥚How to egg?🥚

  • For up to date info, /join eggrun
  • Get to the casino, let people know you're looking for a group
  • When someone opens a portal in casino, hop in! Thank them.
  • When at the teleporter machine, enter the code `0019` (this is what the aquas are for)
  • If you're 70+, talk to Cheb. Cheb is your new friend. Love Cheb.
  • Group leader? Type `/loot unchecked` - Group member? Type `/loot` and make sure you lead set it to unchecked
  • When the door opens, follow the mob of people. Bug murder and egg smashing commence.
  • Do not AOE the big bugs! They have a thorns effect on taking AOE damage that will kill you.
  • Loot knight eggs, smash royal eggs, collect xogrite, loot elites and bosses (Queen/Princess).
  • The average run currently takes Dreva 20m to 30m to finish.
  • After run is completed is book swap!
    • Everyone links the skill books and recipes that dropped for them during the run.
    • If you see something you want, message the person it dropped for.
    • Trades for other wanted items are preferred, but trading equal value books or the vendor value of a book is also acceptable.
    • In the instance multiple people want the same book from a person, an auction or some other method is appropriate to decide.
    • You will likely end up with a bunch of books you don't need. Flia and Cheb are great to sell these books to.
    • Don't expect to say what you're looking for and have everyone check their own inventory for your books - you'll have most success by being proactive.
    • Feel free to offer 2x books if you don't have one of the same value for trade. Most folks are fine with this.
  • Cheb will give extra rewards if you are 70+ once the timer reaches 0. Sub-70s feel free to leave before this.
  • Love egg.
